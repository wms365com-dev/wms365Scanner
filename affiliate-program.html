Program Terms

Simple commission rules.

  • Affiliate earns 25% of monthly WMS365 software subscription fees collected from referred paying customers.
  • Commission is paid within 15 days after WMS365 receives the customer payment.
  • Trial users, unpaid invoices, refunds, failed payments, chargebacks, taxes, pass-through freight, and non-software services are excluded.
  • Referrals should be submitted before or at the time of the first demo, signup, or sales conversation.
  • Affiliates must disclose that they may earn commission when promoting WMS365.

How To Promote

Recommended partner motion.

  • Target 3PLs, ecommerce brands, wholesalers, food and beverage distributors, and warehouses still using Excel.
  • Lead with inventory control, customer portals, receiving, pick/pack/ship flow, billing capture, Shopify, SFTP, lot tracking, and expiration control.
  • Offer a demo handoff to WMS365 instead of trying to configure the warehouse yourself.
  • Use screenshots, the pricing page, and a short workflow story: receive inventory, release an order, pick it, ship it, and bill it.
Affiliate Rules

Keep the program clean.

  • No spam, scraped email blasts, misleading discounts, fake testimonials, or promises not published by WMS365.
  • No bidding on WMS365 brand terms without written approval.
  • No pretending to be WMS365 support, billing, or implementation staff.
  • No self-referrals or referrals from accounts the affiliate owns unless approved in writing.
