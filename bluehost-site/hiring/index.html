Position

WMS365 Sales Member

This is a commission-based sales opportunity for people who can create conversations with warehouse decision-makers and ecommerce operators.

  • Find companies that need warehouse management software, inventory control, order processing, customer portals, integrations, billing capture, or lot/expiration tracking.
  • Introduce prospects to WMS365 and help schedule demos.
  • Follow up with prospects after demos and help move qualified customers toward signup.
  • Stay honest and clear about what WMS365 does today and what requires setup or custom work.
Commission

How the pay works.

  • Sales member earns 25% of monthly WMS365 software subscription fees collected from customers they bring in.
  • Commission is calculated only after customer payment is received by WMS365.
  • Payout target is within 15 days after WMS365 receives customer payment.
  • No commission is earned on failed payments, refunds, chargebacks, taxes, freight pass-through, or one-time non-software services unless approved in writing.

Final role type, tax treatment, and payout agreement will be confirmed in writing before the sales member starts selling.
