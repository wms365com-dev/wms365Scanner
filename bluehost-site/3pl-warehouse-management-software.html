3PL FAQ

Questions warehouse teams usually ask.

Can one customer see another customer's inventory?

No. WMS365 is built to keep customer inventory, orders, and portal visibility scoped to the correct company.

Can different customers have different features?

Yes. Super-user controls let you enable or disable modules by company so one WMS can support different service packages.

Does this fit warehouses that also need integrations and lot control?

Yes. WMS365 can support Shopify, SFTP, lot tracking, and expiration-date logic alongside core 3PL workflows.
