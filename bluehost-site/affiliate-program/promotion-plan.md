# WMS365 Affiliate Program Promotion Plan

## Core offer

Approved affiliates earn 25% of monthly WMS365 software subscription fees collected from referred paying customers. Commission is paid within 15 days after WMS365 receives the customer payment.

Use plain wording when promoting:

> I may earn a commission if you become a paying WMS365 customer through my referral.

## Best-fit promoters

- 3PL consultants and warehouse startup advisors.
- Ecommerce, Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, and marketplace agencies.
- Logistics brokers and fulfillment salespeople who hear customers asking for inventory visibility.
- Zoho, QuickBooks, Dynamics, ERP, and operations consultants.
- Warehouse equipment, barcode, label, packaging, and process-improvement vendors.

## Best-fit buyers

- 3PL warehouses that need customer portals and company-scoped inventory.
- Ecommerce brands using Shopify or marketplaces.
- Small warehouses outgrowing Excel.
- Food, beverage, supplement, health, and consumer products companies that need lot/expiration tracking.
- Operators needing SFTP, order imports, receipt confirmation, shipment confirmation, and billing capture.

## Talking points

- WMS365 is warehouse management software built for 3PL and ecommerce fulfillment.
- The system supports inventory, bin locations, receiving, sales orders, pick tickets, packing slips, customer portals, integrations, lot/expiry tracking, and billing capture.
- Customers can start with a focused core plan and add modules as they grow.
- Grey Wolf 3PL customers can be handled as no-charge 3PL portal users while external warehouse customers can be paywalled.

## Suggested tracking process before a formal affiliate platform

- Affiliate emails referral details to `support@wms365.co` before the first demo or signup.
- WMS365 logs affiliate name, prospect company, contact, date, and source.
- WMS365 confirms acceptance by email.
- Once the customer becomes paying and payment is collected, commission is included in the affiliate payout report.

## Recommended online places to recruit affiliates

- LinkedIn posts and direct outreach to 3PL consultants, ecommerce agencies, Shopify partners, and warehouse consultants.
- PartnerStack Marketplace or similar B2B SaaS partner marketplace once the program is ready to scale.
- Rewardful if WMS365 wants lightweight Stripe-connected affiliate tracking.
- impact.com if WMS365 wants a larger partnership-management platform with broader affiliate, creator, and agency workflows.
- Industry communities: Shopify Partner groups, ecommerce operations forums, 3PL groups, fulfillment consultant newsletters, LinkedIn logistics groups, and warehouse automation communities.

## Compliance guardrails

- Affiliates must clearly disclose that they may earn commission.
- No spam, misleading claims, fake reviews, or pretending to be WMS365.
- No brand keyword advertising without written approval.
- No commission on refunds, chargebacks, taxes, freight pass-through, or unpaid invoices.
