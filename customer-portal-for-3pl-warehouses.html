3PL Customer Portal

Customer portal for 3PL warehouses that keeps every account separate.

WMS365 gives 3PL operators a customer portal where clients can review inventory, orders, inbounds, exports, and shipment activity without seeing another customer's data. It helps warehouses deliver a better customer experience without opening up the full warehouse software to every client.

Why 3PL Portals Matter

Give customers visibility without giving them the warehouse keys.

  • Customers can see only their own inventory, orders, and inbound records
  • Portal activity stays aligned to the same company rules used in the warehouse app
  • Exports, item views, and shipment updates can be packaged as premium services
  • The warehouse team keeps the full operational workspace on the app side

Portal FAQ

Questions 3PL operators usually ask.

Can portal users see other customers?

No. WMS365 is designed so customers can only access the inventory, orders, and records tied to their own account.

Can the portal be packaged as a premium feature?

Yes. Company-level feature controls let super users enable portal access customer by customer.

Does the portal work with exports and order visibility?

Yes. WMS365 can support customer-safe exports and order visibility while keeping the warehouse app separate for internal teams.
