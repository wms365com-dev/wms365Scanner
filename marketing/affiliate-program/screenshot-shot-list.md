## Suggested notes to send with screenshots

- WMS365 keeps customer inventory scoped by company.
- Orders can come from portal entry, Shopify, SFTP, or manual entry.
- Warehouse users can process released orders, print pick tickets, upload documents, and move work through picking, staging, and shipping.
- Billing capture can support processing charges, receiving charges, storage, freight, labels, and service fees.
