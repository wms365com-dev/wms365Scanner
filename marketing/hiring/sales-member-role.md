# WMS365 Sales Member Role

## Position

WMS365 Sales Member

## Role summary

Sales members introduce WMS365 to warehouses, 3PLs, ecommerce brands, distributors, and companies that need better inventory control, customer portals, order processing, integrations, and billing capture.

## Compensation

- 25% recurring monthly commission on WMS365 software subscription fees actually collected from customers the sales member brings in.
- Payout target is within 15 days after WMS365 receives customer payment.
- No commission on failed payments, refunds, chargebacks, taxes, freight pass-through, or one-time non-software services unless approved in writing.

## What the sales member does

- Finds qualified warehouse, 3PL, ecommerce, and inventory-heavy prospects.
- Introduces WMS365 and helps book demos.
- Tracks referral source and customer details before the demo or signup.
- Follows up with prospects and helps move qualified customers toward signup.
- Uses approved WMS365 messaging and avoids promising features or discounts that are not approved.

## Best candidate profile

- Has a warehouse, logistics, ecommerce, software, or B2B sales network.
- Understands basic warehouse pain points such as receiving, inventory accuracy, customer visibility, pick/pack/ship, integrations, billing, lot tracking, and reporting.
- Can sell consultatively and book a demo without overselling.
