Shopify FAQ

Questions brands and fulfillment teams usually ask.

Does WMS365 replace Shopify?

No. WMS365 is the warehouse management layer that can connect to Shopify while keeping warehouse execution and inventory control inside the WMS.

Can Shopify sync be scheduled?

Yes. WMS365 supports manual sync and scheduled sync so orders can be pulled on a warehouse-friendly cadence.

Can a 3PL use this for multiple Shopify customers?

Yes. Each integration record can be tied to the saved company so imported orders stay mapped to the correct customer account.
