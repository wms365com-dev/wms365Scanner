What WMS365 Optimizes

  1. Scanner mode: hardware trigger first on scanner devices, camera on regular phones.
  2. Soft keyboard behavior: hidden for RS60 scan fields so workers are not blocked.
  3. Fast startup: company, locations, SKUs, orders, and counts preload in the background.
  4. Offline support: work queues stay local and sync when connectivity returns.
  5. Consistent UI: same logo, color, large buttons, and compact worker screens.
